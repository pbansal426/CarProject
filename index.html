My Long-Term Project: The 2010 Toyota Corolla

Video Tour

Project Upgrades & Modifications

Interior Luxury & Comfort

The interior was upgraded with new leather seat covers, a wrapped steering wheel, and a refreshed scent for a more comfortable ride.

Modern Entertainment System

A sleek touchscreen head unit with Apple CarPlay replaced the old radio system to enhance the car's entertainment experience.

Safety & Convenience

A rear-view camera wired to the rear-view mirror display improves convenience and safety when reversing.

Improved Visibility & Awareness

Convex blind-spot mirrors and restored headlights enhance visibility and nighttime driving safety.
